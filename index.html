Nobu London 🇬🇧🍣

Nobu is a world-renowned Japanese-Peruvian fusion restaurant that offers a luxurious dining experience. The London location is one of the flagship branches in Europe, attracting celebrities, business elites, and food lovers alike. Located in Mayfair and Shoreditch, Nobu London is famous for its signature dishes like Black Cod with Miso, Yellowtail Jalapeño, and a variety of fresh sushi and sashimi.
